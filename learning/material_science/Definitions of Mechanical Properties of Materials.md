#Material_Science
1. Elasticity
	1. Ability of a material to return to it's original shape after deformation
2. Plasticity
	1. Undergoing plastic deformation without breaking
3. Strength
4. Hardness
	1. Resistance to Plastic Deformation
5. Ductility
	1. Deform under tensile strength
6. Brittleness
	1. Fracture without plastic deformation
7. Toughness
	1. Energy absorption without Fracture 
8. Stiffness
	1. Resistance to Elastic Deformation under load
9. Resilience
	1. Ability of a material to absorb and recover elastic energy
