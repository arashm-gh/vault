# Chapter 2: The Chemical Foundation of Life
1. [[Atom]] $\coloneqq$ Smallest unit of matter that retains all of the element's chemical properties. 
2. Life is made of [[Oxygen]], [[Hydrogen]], [[Carbon]], and [[Nitrogen]].
3. Orbitals and Subshells
	1. Subshell $s$ is sphereical in shape and has one orbital
		1. Holds Max $2$ Electons
	2. Subshell $p$ has three dumbell shaped orbitals
		1. Holds Max 6 Electons
	3. Subshell $d$ has a Cloverleaf shape
		1. Max $5$ Electrons
	4. Subshell $f$ has a complex shape
		1. Max $7$ Electrons
	5. Principal Shell $1n$ 
		1. Has only one $s$ Orbital
		2. Holds $2$ Electrons
	6. Principal Shell $2n$ 
		1. Has one $s$ Orbital and one $p$ Orbital 
		2. Holds $8$ Electrons
	7. Principal Shell $3n$
		1. Has $s$, $p$, and $d$ Subshells
		2. Holds $18$ Electrons
	8. Principal Shell $4n$
		1. Has $s$, $p$, $d$, and $f$ Subshells
		2. Holds $32$ Electrons
	9. Electron Configuration of [[Lithium]]: $1s^22s^1$
		1. 2 Electrons in the $1n$ Shell's $s$ orbital
		2. 1 Electron in the $2n$ Shell's $s$ orbital
	10. $\ce{ H\bond{1}O\bond{1}H }$
