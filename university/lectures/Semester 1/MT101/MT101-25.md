4. Word Problem: Studies have shown students learn new vocabulary at a rate which decreases as the size of the dictionary increases. The number of words learned, $W(t)$, in $t$ days is given: $$W'(t) = \frac{8200}{W(t)}\,for\, 0\leq t\leq365$$ Find $W(t)$ at $W(0) = 400$. Find $W(2)$
	1. $W(t)W'(t) = 8200$
	2. $\int W(t)W'(t) dt = \int 8200 dt$
	3. $u = W(t), du = W'(t)dt$
	4. $\int u du = 8200t +C$
	5. $\frac{W(t)^2}{2} = 8200t +C$
	6. $W(t) = \sqrt{16400t +2C}$
	7. $\sqrt{2C} = 400, C = 80000$
	8. $W(t) = \sqrt{16400t + 160000}$
	9. $W(t) = 20\sqrt{41t+400}$
	10. $W(2) = 20\sqrt{82+400} = 20\sqrt{482} \approx 439$
