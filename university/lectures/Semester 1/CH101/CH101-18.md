	1. Composition
		1. 85% [[Methane]], 9% [[Ethane]], 3% [[Propane]], 1% [[Butane]], 1% [[Nitrogen]], traces of $CO_2$, $H_2S$, moisture
		2. [[Monoethanolamine]], [[Diethanolamine]], [[Methyldiethanolamine]] = amine-based solvents
			1. Absorb $CO_2$, $H_2S$
		3. Odorant called [[Mercapton]] is added for leak detection
		4. Methane requires significant processing before use but is one of the cleanest-burning fossil fuels
	2. Natural Gas Purification
		1. Removal of Solid Impurities
			1. Dust, Sand, and other Particulates are removed using Filters or Mechanical Separators
		2. Removal of Acid Gases (Sweetening) 
			1. Acidic and Toxic Gases like $H_2S$ and $CO_2$ are removed using [[Diethanolamine]] and [[Methyldiethanolamine]] - Ionic Bond
		3. Moisture Removal/Dehydration
			1. [[Diethylene Glycol]] high affinity for water - Hydrogen Bond
		4. Removal of Heavy Hydrocarbons
			1. [[Ethane]], [[Propane]], and [[Butane]] are absorbed using [[Hexane]]
		5. [[Fractional Distillation]]
			1. Used to recover Hexane and separate hydrocarbons
